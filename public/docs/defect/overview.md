# Defect — Character Overview

The **Defect** is a sentient automaton that channels **Orbs** — elemental constructs that generate passive effects each turn and trigger a powerful **Evoke** when removed from the orb queue. Mastering the Defect means mastering orb management: knowing when to channel, when to evoke, and how to scale **Focus** so your orbs do more work with less energy.

## Playstyle

The Defect is the most mechanically unique character. Instead of scaling through stat buffs alone, you're managing a queue of up to 3–5 orb slots, filling it with different orb types depending on what you need — Lightning for burst damage, Frost for block, Dark for building up a devastating Evoke hit, and Plasma for bonus energy every turn.

He rewards planning over reaction. The best Defect turns are ones where you engineered the orb queue two turns ago.

## The Four Orbs

| Orb | Passive | Evoke |
|-----|---------|-------|
| ⚡ **Lightning** | Deal 3 damage to a random enemy | Deal 8 damage to all enemies |
| ❄️ **Frost** | Gain 2 Block | Gain 5 Block |
| 🌑 **Dark** | Add 6 damage (stacks) | Deal all accumulated damage to lowest-HP enemy |
| 🔮 **Plasma** | Gain 1 Energy | Gain 2 Energy |

*All values above are base stats. Focus increases Passive/Evoke values by 1 each per point of Focus.*

## Focus — The Core Stat

**Focus** is the Defect's primary scaling stat. Every point of Focus adds 1 to both the Passive and Evoke values of every orb. At 3 Focus, Lightning deals 6 passive damage per turn and evokes for 11 to all enemies. At 5 Focus, Frost passively generates 7 block per turn — more than most Defend cards.

Prioritise Focus-generating cards and relics whenever you can.

## Strengths

| Strength | Why It Matters |
|----------|---------------|
| Passive damage/block | Orbs generate value every turn without spending cards or energy |
| Extremely flexible archetypes | Lightning, Frost, Dark, and Plasma builds all feel entirely different |
| Strong vs. high-HP targets | Dark orbs accumulate and release massive single-target damage |
| Scales harder than almost anyone | High Focus + full orb slots = enormous passive turns |

## Weaknesses

- **Orb slots are a limited resource** — you start with 3 slots. Channelling a new orb pushes out the oldest one, which can accidentally Evoke at the wrong moment.
- **Relies on synergy to shine** — a random mix of orb types without Focus is just okay. Without commitment the Defect feels mediocre.
- **Weak to debuffs that reduce orb slots** — some enemies and bosses shrink your orb queue, gutting your passive income.

## Early Priority Picks

- **Glacier** — 2 energy, channel 2 Frost. Incredible value in Frost/Focus builds.
- **Ball Lightning** — 1 energy, deal 7 and channel Lightning. An efficient workhorse.
- **Chill** — 0 energy, channel Frost. Free block generation is always welcome.
- **Charge Battery** — channel a Plasma, gain Energy next turn. Fixes energy issues.
- **Consume** — lose 1 orb slot, gain 2 Focus permanently. Best taken when you're already playing efficiently with fewer orb types.

## Tips

> **Don't fill all your orb slots with one type blindly.** A mix of Frost (for block) and Lightning (for damage) is often more resilient than all Lightning — you don't get caught dead in a high-damage turn with no block.

> **Dark orbs are deceptively powerful.** In the early game they seem weak, but against bosses with 200+ HP, a fully stacked Dark orb with 3+ Focus can Evoke for 100+ damage in a single burst.
