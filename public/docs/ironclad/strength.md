# Ironclad — Strength Build

The Strength Build is the Ironclad's most explosive archetype. The goal is simple: stack Strength as fast as possible and watch your cards deal three to five times their printed damage values. A well-executed Strength run can one-shot elites and shrug off the final boss.

## Core Cards

| Card | Role |
|------|------|
| **Inflame** | +2 Strength every time you play it. Stack multiples if you can. |
| **Limit Break** | Double your current Strength (Exhaust). The single most powerful Strength card in the game when upgraded. |
| **Heavy Blade** | Base 14 damage, but Strength applies **3×**. With even 6 Strength this deals 32 — before upgrades. |
| **Spot Weakness** | Gain 3 Strength when you attack an Elite. Free value that pays for itself every elite fight. |
| **Demon Form** | Gain 2 Strength at the start of every turn. The premier late-game scaling engine. |

## Key Relics

- **Paper Phrog** — Gain 2 Strength after every combat. Turns a middling run into a monster by Act 3.
- **Vajra** — Start every combat with 1 Strength. Synergises with everything.
- **Red Skull** — Gain 3 Strength when you drop below 50% HP. High risk, high reward. Pairs beautifully with Reaper.
- **Wrist Blade** — Deal 4 extra damage with 0-cost attacks. Helps in shiv-adjacent setups but also pairs with free-cost Shrug It Off+ or Burning Pact.

## Build Path

### Act 1
Focus on picking up at least **two Strength sources** before the boss. Inflame is the safest. Spot Weakness is free if you're fighting elites. Avoid bloating your deck — keep it to 10–14 cards maximum entering Act 2.

### Act 2
This is where the build accelerates. Look for:
- **Limit Break** — your win condition if you have 6+ Strength by mid-Act 2.
- **Demon Form** — take it even if it feels slow. The compounding turns it into an unstoppable engine.
- **Heavy Blade** — should already be in your deck. Upgrade it immediately if you haven't.

### Act 3
Your deck should be hitting for 50–100+ damage per swing by now. The main threats are enemies that apply **Weak** (halves your attack damage) and enemies that **Shrink your hand size**. Keep Shrug It Off or a few block cards in reserve.

## Common Pitfalls

> **Don't run Limit Break without enough base Strength.** Doubling 2 Strength is just 4 Strength. Doubling 8 is 16. Wait until you have a meaningful Strength stack before committing to the Limit Break combo.

> **Watch out for Intangible bosses.** The Strength build relies entirely on damage, and encounters that make you deal 1 damage per hit (like the Act 3 boss) can catch you off-guard without a backup plan.

## Example Turn 3 (ideal state)

Assuming Inflame ×2 + Demon Form in deck, with 4 Strength from prior turns:

1. Start of turn: +2 Strength from Demon Form → now at **6 Strength**
2. Play Inflame → **8 Strength**
3. Play Heavy Blade (3× Strength): `14 + (8×3)` = **38 damage**
4. Play Limit Break → **16 Strength**
5. Next turn's Heavy Blade: `14 + (16×3)` = **62 damage**
