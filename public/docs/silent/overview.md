# Silent — Character Overview

The **Silent** is a fast, technical character who can win with anything as slow as hard stall to anything as fast as infinite loops. She starts with 70 HP, the lowest in the game, but compensates with the most powerful card draw and one of the deadliest win conditions in Slay The Spire 2, **Poison**

## Playstyle

The Silent is all about **volume**, she floods the combat with cheap cards and stacking damage-over-time through Poison. Her starting relic *Ring of the Snake* draws 2 extra cards at the start of combat, which sets the tone for how she plays every fight.

## Strengths

| Strength | Why It Matters |
|----------|---------------|
| Best card draw in the game | Rarely dead in hand, cycles to key pieces consistently |
| Multiple strong archetypes | Poison, Shivs, Discard; any of the three can carry a run |
| Strong early survivability | Backflip, Survivor, Blade Dance are all excellent early picks |

## Weaknesses

- **Low HP (70)** — mistakes are punished harder than usual. You can't afford to take 15+ damage per turn and expect to recover.
- **No Reliable Healing Outside of Rest Spots** — Ditto the above, but even more so. 
- **Higher Skill Floor** — The Silent demands more card management and gamesense than most of her compatriots. Getting into an infinite loop is possible, but it will be nonlinear. 

## Early Priority Picks

- **Acrobatics** — Fantastic draw option that also procs your sly effects.
- **Prepared** — 0 cost draw 1 discard 1 is good early and shines even more when upgraded.
- **Tactician** — The enabler to end all enablers, energy regeneration is exceptionally strong.
- **Piercing Wail** — Helps you get through fights with multiple enemies. Exhausts itself late game
- **Well-laid Plans** — An unassuming but powerful card that thins your deck, high skill ceiling.

## Tips

> **Ring of the Snake is strongest in short combats.** You'll draw 2 extra cards on your first turn every fight. This allows you to get off to the races more consistently and combos amazingly with relics like Very Hot Cocoa. 

> **Don't overlook discard synergy.** Any cards with sly are worth considering in the very early game. Ricochet specifically is a common that has a strong synergy with any of the card modifiers that increase damage.
