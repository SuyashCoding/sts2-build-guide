# Silent — Character Overview

The **Silent** is a fast, technical character who wins through attrition and resource efficiency rather than raw brute force. She starts with 70 HP — the lowest in the game — but compensates with the most powerful card draw, the widest range of defensive tools, and two of the deadliest win conditions in any Slay the Spire run: **Poison** and **Shivs**.

## Playstyle

The Silent is all about **volume**. Where the Ironclad lands a few massive hits, she floods the combat with cheap cards, tiny hits that add up, and stacking damage-over-time through Poison. Her starting relic *Ring of the Snake* draws 2 extra cards at the start of combat — before anything else happens — which sets the tone for how she plays every fight.

She rewards players who think ahead. Her best turns aren't "play this one card that wins" — they're carefully sequenced chains where each card enables the next.

## Strengths

| Strength | Why It Matters |
|----------|---------------|
| Best card draw in the game | Rarely dead in hand, cycles to key pieces consistently |
| Multiple strong archetypes | Poison, Shivs, Discard — any of the three can carry a run |
| Strong early survivability | Backflip, Survivor, Blade Dance are all excellent early picks |
| Excellent defensive options | Blur, Footwork, and Apparition relics all make her surprisingly tanky |

## Weaknesses

- **Low HP (70)** — mistakes are punished harder than with the Ironclad. You can't afford to take 15+ damage per turn and expect to recover.
- **Scaling is slow without commitment** — the Silent doesn't have one "always good" win condition. You need to read what the run is offering and commit to it early.
- **Poison requires patience** — stacking Poison before an enemy attacks is a skill in itself. Getting the timing wrong wastes stacks.

## Early Priority Picks

- **Deadly Poison** — 2 energy for 5 Poison is the backbone of any poison build. Always consider.
- **Blade Dance** — generates 3 Shivs for 1 energy. A great pick even outside a dedicated Shiv build.
- **Backflip** — draw 2, gain 3 block. One of the best early cards in the game at any ascension.
- **Accuracy** — if you're taking Shivs anywhere, this card multiplies their damage. Take it early.
- **Burst** — next skill plays twice. Combines with everything. A build-definer.

## Tips

> **Ring of the Snake is strongest in short combats.** You'll draw 2 extra cards in every fight regardless of length, but in quick fights those cards matter enormously — you may draw your entire hand before the enemy attacks.

> **Don't overlook discard synergy.** Many Silent cards trigger when you discard — Reflex draws cards, Calculated Gamble redraws your whole hand, and Sneaky Strike deals damage. If you pick up Acrobatics or Survivor early, consider building around it.
